In the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit, one God, Amen! The purpose of this project is to solve a problem that I and millions of other Orthodox Christians deal with on a nearly daily basis, finding out the date. Both the Oriental and Eastern Orthodox Churches use a unique calendar, called the Julian or Alexandrian Calendar. The struggle that comes with it though is that it's completely different than the traditionally used, Gregorian calendar. This app's main goal is to solve that very problem. To create a calendar that can convert any Julian date, in numerical form, to the Gregorian Calendar. It can also save dates and holidays. For example, in the Oriental Orthodox church, nearly every day each month there is a monthly or yearly celebration of a Saint and we are called to pray asking for their intersessions. This app informs the user on the Saints of that day, letting users grow spiritually. With that being said, like what the Heart of the Lord King David said, we should be like the Saints, who are "double-edged swords" (Psalm 149:6) and not only focus on our spiritual life's, but also our careers, but specifically our school. The app also lets you use it as a planner. Instead of a holiday, you can make it so you replace the holiday name with a reminder.
